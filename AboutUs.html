About Us

NV22179 - Hassan Naeem

I am Hassan, and my journey at the Nasser Center for Science and Technology (NCST) began after I completed middle school. I was curious about future job opportunities and the skills required to secure them. Intrigued by the diverse courses offered at NCST, I decided to enroll but was initially unsure about which major to choose. My passion for building computers and coding led me to explore both engineering and IT during my first semester. This foundational experience helped me realize that artificial intelligence (AI) was the perfect blend of my interests, as it combines coding with the creation of Internet of Things (IoT) devices, much like working with computers.

At NCST, I am majoring in AI, where we delve into fascinating subjects like Machine Learning and Deep Learning. These courses have opened my eyes to the incredible potential of AI in transforming various industries. My favorite subject, however, is English. I thoroughly enjoy it, especially because of my favorite teacher, Mr. Scott, who makes learning engaging and fun. His classes have significantly improved my communication skills, which are essential in any field, including AI.

The school itself offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide range of subjects, ensuring that students receive a well-rounded education. The supportive environment at NCST has allowed me to enhance my skills in coding and robotics, providing me with hands-on experience that is invaluable for my future career. The faculty members are knowledgeable and always willing to help, making the learning experience both challenging and rewarding.

Looking ahead, I aim to attend a university that offers advanced courses in data science and artificial intelligence. My ultimate goal is to become a certified Artificial Intelligence Engineer, contributing to innovative projects that can make a significant impact on society. The journey at NCST has been instrumental in shaping my career path, and I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead.

NV22090 - Ali Jaffer

My first time entering the school, I was extremely nervous, imagining high school students based on what I had seen in movies, such as emotionally unstable teens or seniors who always take risks and go out with friends. When I entered, I saw many people, some whose personalities were accurate to the movies, and some who had unique styles. After seeing many interesting people, I hoped to make new friends and interact with my teachers and colleagues, but it was not as easy as expected. The students in my year already had friends from their previous schools or the neighborhood, but the teachers were very supportive and made the school a great experience throughout the year. When the final exams got closer and closer, I was very shocked to see that there were two exams on each day, but I managed to pull through with excellent marks.

In my sophomore year, I got a pretty good idea about how the school works and how to interact with students and teachers. I chose my major, which is Artificial Intelligence. I earned many certificates this year, including a Python programming competition and honorable student awards. This year was very smooth as I had spent a year with my colleagues and got very close with them.

Now I am in my senior year, having spent two years with my colleagues and made very good friends with them, which made the school very enjoyable. Now I have plenty of experience, which I gained from going to events and exhibitions with the school, giving a presentation at one of them. These experiences all help with preparing me for my on-the-job training. To prepare for on-the-job training and university, I am aiming to build my curriculum vitae (CV) by taking online courses, getting certificates, and studying for my SAT.

Overall, my journey in NCST so far has been unique and amazing. I have enjoyed it a lot. From the experience I gained and the friends I made along the way, it has been an invaluable experience.
