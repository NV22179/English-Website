AI turned out to be really helpful, especially when I was studying for exams, as it helped me review things more quickly by explaining them in a way that made sense to me. However, when I first started using AI, it wasn’t as advanced as it is now, and I sometimes encountered wrong information.

Currently, I use ChatGPT for general questions Claude for its accuracy and speed, and NoteGPT to extract notes from YouTube videos, which helps me save time and manage my studies better. These tools have really helped boost my grades by making studying more effective. I’ve realized that AI is there to support me, not to do the work for me, so I use it for ideas and direction instead of just copying what it says. All in all, AI has been a fantastic help in my education, significantly changing how I learn and succeed.

NV22090 - Ali Jaffer

NCST fighting deforestation and pollution. At NCST, we use a sustainable way of learning by conducting tests and assignments online through laptops or iPad’s instead of paper which piles up and ends up in the garbage. This change has a significant impact on the environment, imagine 500 students using more than 3 papers each every day that is many papers which costs many trees, trees are important for our environment by absorbing carbon dioxide, releasing oxygen, and maintain biodiversity. This helps preserve trees which are an essential resource, but it doesn’t end here, conducting online test and assignments lowers waste majorly as paper waste causes pollution which often ends up in landfills or being incinerated, which releases harmful chemicals in the air. This digital change cuts down significantly on this environmental impact. Using tech for assignments and test is more convenient as there is no need to worry about misplaced papers or running out of pages while everything is on one device. Using tech is not easy for everyone this could encourage them to learn and understand modern devices as digital work is the way for the future, while technical issues could arise and cause problems these issues could be solved with ease by an expert and the environmental benefits outweigh these minor inconveniences. By making this shift, NCST is not only helping the environment but setting an example for others to follow. This is important as this small change by one school could contribute to changes on a larger scale and cause a healthier planet.
